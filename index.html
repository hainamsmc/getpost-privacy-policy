Privacy Policy for GetPost

At GetPost, accessible from https://getpost. NeihooStore, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by GetPost and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. However, we do collect detailed information from posts on fan pages that you retrieve using the app.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to improve the functionality of our app and provide you with relevant and accurate data.

Log Files

GetPost follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, GetPost uses cookies to store information about visitors' preferences and the pages they accessed or visited. This information is used to optimize the user experience.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

GetPost's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We encourage you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information.
